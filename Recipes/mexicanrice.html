Alicia's Mexican Rice

Ingredients

  • 3-4 Tomatoes
  • Yellow Onion
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • Chicken Broth
  • Rice
  • Oil
  • Peas and Carrots (optional)

Directions

  1. After rinsing the rice, put it in a pot with some oil.
  2. Brown the rice.
  3. Blend the tomatoes, half the onion, garlic and chicken broth.
  4. Add liquid and peas and carrots to the pot.
  5. Cover until cooked through.
